THE SESSION

Three steps, twenty minutes, no equipment to figure out.

This is a real sequence — you move through it in order, every visit.

01 / SUIT UP

Your trainer fits the suit

No gym clothes needed — a sterilized EMS suit goes on in under five minutes, positioned to your body.

02 / TRAIN

20 minutes, low-impact movement

Simple guided exercises while impulses do the deep work — easy on joints and tendons, hard on muscle fibers.

03 / RECOVER & REPEAT

Most clients train twice a week

Paired with nutrition guidance, that's usually enough to see real change inside a month.

FIND US

Slim Fit EMS — fitness, nutrition, and pain relief, all in 20 minutes.

Slim Fit EMS is an Electrical Muscle Stimulation training gym with two branches in Cairo — Nasr City and Heliopolis. Every session is guided by a trainer, uses a suit that's fitted to you, and runs just 20 minutes.

Beyond the workout, we support clients with nutrition guidance and pain-relief sessions for chronic muscle and joint pain — not just fitness goals.
